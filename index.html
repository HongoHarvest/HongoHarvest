Our Story

Cultivating Zambia's Agricultural Future

Founded in 2015, HongoHarvest has been at the forefront of agricultural innovation in Zambia. Our mission is to empower local farmers through sustainable practices, modern technology, and community-driven solutions.

We believe that the future of agriculture lies in balancing productivity with environmental stewardship. Our team of experts works closely with farmers across the country to implement practices that increase yields while preserving our precious natural resources.

Our Mission

To revolutionize Zambian agriculture through sustainable practices that benefit farmers, communities, and the environment.

Our Vision

A Zambia where agriculture thrives as an environmentally sustainable and economically viable sector for all.
