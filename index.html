About HongoHarvest

Driving agricultural innovation in Zambia since 2010

Our Mission

HongoHarvest empowers Zambian farmers through sustainable practices, modern technology, and community partnerships. We bridge traditional knowledge with innovative solutions to transform agriculture across the nation.

Sustainability

Eco-friendly farming practices that preserve Zambia's natural resources

Economic Growth

Increasing farmer incomes through value chain development

Community

Building farmer cooperatives and knowledge-sharing networks

Innovation

Research-driven solutions for Zambia's unique challenges
