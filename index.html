Innovative Agriculture For Inclusive Growth

Our Mission

We are on a mission to develop high-impact agricultural zones powered by water reservoirs and sustainable irrigation.

Our Vision

Empowering youth and women through agri-employment and entrepreneurial training. Create agro-processing and tech hubs that drive value addition and rural industrialization.

Our Commitment

Driving Sustainable Change

At HongoHarvest, we believe that sustainable agriculture is the foundation of thriving communities. By integrating traditional knowledge with modern innovations, we're creating resilient farming systems that withstand climate challenges while providing economic opportunities for Zambia's youth and women.

Our holistic approach combines water conservation, renewable energy, and market access to create a self-sustaining agricultural ecosystem that benefits both people and the planet.

Our Methodology

How We Work

Our Integrated Approach

We combine traditional farming wisdom with modern technology to create sustainable solutions that benefit both farmers and the environment.

Water Management

Developing sustainable irrigation systems and water reservoirs to ensure year-round farming even in drought-prone regions.

Farmer Training

Empowering farmers with knowledge on climate-smart agriculture, sustainable practices, and modern farming techniques.

Renewable Energy

Implementing solar-powered solutions for irrigation and processing to reduce carbon footprint and operational costs.

Market Access

Creating direct connections between farmers and markets to ensure fair prices and sustainable income streams.

Climate-Smart Agriculture

We implement climate-resilient farming practices that adapt to changing weather patterns while reducing agriculture's environmental impact. Our approach includes:

Drought-resistant crop varieties that thrive in changing climate conditions

Soil conservation techniques to prevent erosion and maintain fertility

Precision irrigation systems that optimize water usage

Integrated pest management reducing chemical dependency

Agroforestry practices that enhance biodiversity

Sustainable crop rotation systems to maintain soil health
